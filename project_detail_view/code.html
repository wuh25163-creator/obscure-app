The Concept

The Obscure project challenges traditional luxury codes by stripping away ornament in favor of raw geometric presence. Rooted in the Bauhaus principle of "less is more," the design system focuses on the tension between rough textures and surgical precision.

Our objective was to create a visual language that feels ancient yet digital, utilizing high-contrast typographic grids that dictate the form of the physical packaging itself.
